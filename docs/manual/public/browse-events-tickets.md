## Events

The public [Events](/events) page shows upcoming shows and past events. Each event
displays its poster, date, and ticket info. Filter by tag to find the kind of show
you're after.

## Buying tickets as a guest

When an event sells tickets, you can buy them without an account:

1. Open the event and start the ticket purchase.
2. Enter your details and pay through the secure checkout.
3. You'll get a confirmation by email.

## Why sign in

With a [free account](create-account.md), your tickets are saved under **My
Tickets**, you get a scannable QR for check-in, and any member discount is applied
automatically.
