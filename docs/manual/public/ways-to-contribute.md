## Supporting CorvMC

The [Contribute](/contribute) page lays out how you can support the collective.

## Become a sustaining member

Choose a contribution tier (for example $5, $25, or $100+) and click **Choose** to
get started. Sustaining membership funds the space and unlocks benefits like free
practice hours. You'll be guided to sign in or create an account.

## Other ways to help

- **Donate gear** — Contribute instruments or equipment to the lending library.
- **Volunteer** — Lend a hand with events and operations.
- **Host a workshop** — Share your skills with the community.

These options link to the [contact form](contact-and-perform.md) so we can follow
up with you.

## Already a member?

Manage your sustaining membership any time from your member area.
