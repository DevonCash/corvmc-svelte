## What is CorvMC?

The Corvallis Music Collective (CorvMC) is a community music space. We offer
affordable rehearsal rooms, a directory of local musicians and bands, community
events and shows, and an equipment lending library — supported by members and
sustaining contributions.

## Getting around

From the public site you can:

- **Programs** — Learn what we offer: practice space, shows, and meetups/clubs.
- **Directory** — Browse local musicians and bands.
- **Events** — See upcoming shows and buy tickets.
- **Contribute** — Support the collective or apply to perform.
- **Contact** — Get in touch.

## Joining

Click **Join Our Community** (or **Sign in**) to create a free account. With an
account you can book practice space, build a profile, join bands, borrow gear, and
manage tickets. See [Create an account](create-account.md) to get started.
