## The public directory

Anyone can browse the [Directory](/directory) — no account needed. Switch between
two tabs:

- **Musicians** — Cards showing each player's instruments, genres, and whether
  they're looking to collaborate, for hire, or teaching.
- **Bands** — Cards for local bands with their genres and links.

## Finding people

Use the filters and search to narrow by instrument or genre. Click any card to open
the full profile, where you'll find a bio, links, and any contact info that person
has chosen to make public.

## What you can't see signed out

Some members set their profile to **members-only**, so a few listings only appear
once you're signed in. [Create an account](create-account.md) to see the full
community — and to add your own listing.
