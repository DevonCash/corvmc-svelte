## Signing in

Go to the [sign-in page](/login). Enter your email and password to sign in. Use the
show/hide toggle to check your password as you type.

## Registering

On the same page, switch to **Register** to create a free account:

1. Enter your **name**, **email**, and a **password** (at least 8 characters).
2. Submit to create your account.
3. You'll land in your member area.

## Joining from a band invite

If a band invited you by email, opening that invite link brings you to the sign-in
page pre-filled with the invitation details. Register or sign in with that email and
the band invitation will be waiting for you to accept.

## After signing in

You're taken to your member dashboard. From there, fill out your profile, book
practice space, and explore events.
