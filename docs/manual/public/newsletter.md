## Subscribing

Visit [Subscribe](/subscribe) to join a mailing list:

1. Choose a **mailing list** from the options.
2. Enter your **email** (and optionally your **name**).
3. Subscribe.

You don't need an account to subscribe. A direct link to a specific list looks like
`/subscribe/<list>`.

## Unsubscribing

Every email we send includes an **unsubscribe** link at the bottom — one click
removes you from that list. The link takes you to a page that confirms you're
unsubscribed.

## If you have an account

Signed-in members manage all their list subscriptions in one place under **Account
→ Email subscriptions**.
