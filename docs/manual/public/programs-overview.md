## Our programs

The [Programs](/programs) page covers everything CorvMC offers.

## Practice space

Affordable, bookable rehearsal rooms. Sustaining members get free practice hours
each month; others pay an hourly rate. [Create an account](create-account.md) to
book.

## Shows & performances

CorvMC hosts and supports live shows. If you'd like to perform, use **Apply to
Perform**, which takes you to the [contact form](contact-and-perform.md).

## Meetups & clubs

Recurring community gatherings — for example a Real Book Club, a Songwriter Circle,
and a monthly meetup. These are open ways to connect with other musicians.
