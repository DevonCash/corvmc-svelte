## The contact form

Visit [Contact](/contact) to send us a message. Fill in:

- **Name** and **email** so we can reply.
- A **subject** — choose the option that fits (general inquiry, performing, etc.).
- Your **message**.

The page also lists our address and contact details, and a short FAQ for common
questions.

## Applying to perform

Want to play a show at CorvMC? Use the contact form and choose the
performance-related subject (you can also reach it from the **Apply to Perform**
link on the [Programs](programs-overview.md) page). Tell us about your act, links to
your music, and your availability, and we'll follow up.

## Where it goes

Messages land in the staff inbox, and staff reply by email.
