What to expect

Real Book Club is an open jam, not a performance. The first hour we work through 2–3 standards as a group — pick a key, count it off, take turns soloing. The second hour is more freeform: bring requests, sit in on whatever's happening, or just listen.

New to standards? We'll share a Real Book and walk you through it. New to jazz? Even better — half the room was in your seat last year.
