## Electronic Press Kit (EPK)

Premium bands get an EPK editor for the information venues, bookers, and press
need. Open it from **Page Editor → EPK**. The data you enter feeds your page's
Press, Contact, and Tech Rider blocks.

## What to fill in

- **Contacts** — Separate booking, management, and press contacts.
- **Press quotes** — Testimonials and review pull-quotes.
- **Backline / stage requirements** — What you need on stage.
- **Achievements** — Notable highlights.

## Saving

Your EPK is saved with the band page. Keep it current — it's often the first thing
a booker looks at.
