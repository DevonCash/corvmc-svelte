## Media for your page

The **Page Editor** has a media section for uploading the images and files your
blocks use:

- **Gallery** images for the Gallery block.
- **Hero** art for the Hero block.
- **Stage plot** and **tech rider** files for the Tech Rider block and your
  [press kit](/member/help/electronic-press-kit).

## Uploading

Upload files in the media section, then reference them from the relevant block.
Use clear, reasonably sized images so the page loads quickly and looks sharp.
