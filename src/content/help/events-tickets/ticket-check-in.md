## Your ticket stubs

Every ticket you buy shows up under **My Tickets** on the **Events** page. Each one
is a stub for a specific event.

## Checking in

1. At the event, open **Events → My Tickets**.
2. Tap the ticket stub for that event.
3. A **QR code** appears — show it to the staff member at the door.

Staff scan the code to check you in. The QR is tied to your ticket, so there's
nothing to print.

## Tips

- Pull up the QR before you reach the door in case the venue's signal is weak.
- If you bought multiple tickets, each has its own stub and QR.
