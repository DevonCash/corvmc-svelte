## Buying a ticket

1. Open **Events** and choose a ticketed event.
2. Start the ticket purchase.
3. Complete the secure card checkout.

As a signed-in member, your details are already known, and any member discount is
applied automatically at checkout.

## After purchase

Your ticket appears under **My Tickets** on the Events page as a stub. When you
arrive, [show your ticket's QR code](/member/help/ticket-check-in) to check in.

A confirmation is also sent to your email.

## Buying without an account

Anyone can buy tickets as a guest from the public events page — an account isn't
required — but signing in keeps all your tickets in one place and applies member
pricing.
