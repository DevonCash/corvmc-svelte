## Events

Open **Events** from the sidebar to see upcoming shows and community events, plus
a record of past ones.

## On the page

- **Filters** — Narrow events by tag (genre or type).
- **Event posters** — Each event shows its poster, date, and details.
- **My Tickets** — Tickets you've bought appear here as stubs.

## What you can do

- [Buy a ticket](/member/help/buy-a-ticket) for an upcoming event.
- [Show your ticket / QR at the door](/member/help/ticket-check-in) to check in.
- Open any event for full details — time, location, and lineup.

Not all events are ticketed; some are free to attend.
