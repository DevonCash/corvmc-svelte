## Avatar

On your **Profile** page, upload an avatar image. This is the photo shown on your
directory card and next to your name across the site. A clear, square image works
best.

## Tagline

Your **tagline** is a single line shown directly on your directory card — think of
it as your headline. Keep it short and specific, e.g. _"Drummer, into post-rock,
looking for a band."_

## Bio

The **bio** is a longer description with a rich-text editor. Use it to introduce
yourself: your background, influences, what you're looking for, and anything that
helps the right people reach out.

Save when you're done (`Mod+S` works too).
