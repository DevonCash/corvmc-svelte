## Status flags

Your profile has a set of toggles that tell other members what you're open to.
Turn on the ones that apply:

- **Looking for a band** — You want to join or form a band.
- **Available for hire** — You'll play paid gigs or sessions.
- **Teaches lessons** — You offer instruction.
- **Open to collaboration** — You're up for one-off or project-based work.

## How they're used

These flags appear on your directory card and help others quickly see how to work
with you. They also feed directory searches, so members looking specifically for,
say, a teacher or a hired player can find you.

Toggle them on your **Profile** page and save. You can change them any time as your
situation changes.
