## Instruments

On your **Profile** page, add the instruments you play. As you type, suggestions
appear — pick an existing one to stay consistent with how others are tagged, or
add your own. List everything you'd want to be found for.

## Genres

Add genres the same way. These describe the styles you play or are interested in.
Genres and instruments are what other members filter by in the directory, so the
more accurate they are, the better your matches.

## Why it matters

Members searching the [directory](/member/help/find-members-bands) can narrow by
instrument and genre. Good tags are the difference between being found and being
missed.
