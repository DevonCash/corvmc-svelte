## Links

On your **Profile** page, add links to your music and online presence — YouTube,
Spotify, SoundCloud, Bandcamp, Instagram, a personal site, and so on. These show
on your profile so people can hear and follow you. Add as many as are relevant.

## Directory contact info

Separately, you can add **directory contact info** — a public email, phone number,
or social handle — that other members can use to reach you directly.

This is optional and independent of your account email. Only add what you're
comfortable showing publicly, and use [visibility settings](/member/help/directory-visibility)
to control who can see your profile at all.
