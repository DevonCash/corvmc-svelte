## Payments

**Staff → Payments** is the record of all transactions. Search by member and filter
by payment method, status, and date range. Each row shows the date, member, amount,
method, status, and Stripe reference.

## Credits

**Staff → Credits** is the ledger of credit activity — free practice hours and
equipment credits. Search by member and filter by credit type and source to see
where credits came from and how they were used.

## Reconciling

- Use payments to confirm a charge or locate a Stripe transaction.
- Use the credit ledger to verify a member's balance or investigate a discrepancy.
- Adjust an individual member's balance from their
  [user detail page](/member/help/staff-edit-user).
