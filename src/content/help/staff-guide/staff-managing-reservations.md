## The reservations list

**Staff → Reservations** shows every booking. Switch between **Upcoming** and
**All**, search by member name or email, and filter by date range. Each row shows
the member, time, booker type, and payment status.

## Actions

- **Confirm** — Approve a pending or waitlisted reservation.
- **Complete** — Mark a session done after it happens.
- **No-show** — Flag when the member didn't arrive.
- **Cancel** — Cancel with an optional reason.

## Resolving issues

Use the **Resolve** panel to handle reservations that are past their end time but
not yet completed. Open any row to reach the
[detail page](/member/help/staff-reservation-payments) for payment handling and a
day-timeline view of the slot.
