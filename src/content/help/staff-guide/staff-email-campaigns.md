## Campaigns

**Staff → Marketing → Campaigns** lists your email campaigns with their subject,
status (Draft, Scheduled, Sent), audiences, recipient count, and date.

## Creating a campaign

1. Choose **New Campaign**.
2. Write the **subject** and the **body** (markdown with a live preview).
3. Select one or more [audiences](/member/help/staff-audiences) to send to.
4. **Preview** and send a **test email** to yourself first.
5. **Send now** or **schedule** it for a later time.

## Editing and viewing

- Drafts open in the editor so you can keep working on them.
- Sent campaigns open read-only with their delivery details.

## Tips

- Always send a test to check formatting and links before a real send.
- Double-check the selected audiences and recipient count before sending.
