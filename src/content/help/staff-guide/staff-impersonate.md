## Impersonation

When helping a member, you can **impersonate** them to see exactly what they see.
Start it from the actions menu on the **Users** list, or from their detail page.

## Using it responsibly

- Impersonate only to **diagnose a support issue**, not to act as the member
  without cause.
- You're seeing their account — be mindful of their private data.
- End impersonation when you're done to return to your own session.

## When to use it

- A member reports something "isn't showing up" — check from their view.
- Confirming a booking, ticket, or permission issue specific to that account.
