## The staff area

The **Staff** panel is where you run day-to-day operations. The dashboard opens
with at-a-glance stats — total members, active roles, permissions, and new members
this month — plus a table of recently joined members.

## What's in the sidebar

- **Reservations** — Manage all practice-space bookings and payments.
- **Recurring** — Standing reservation series.
- **Events** — Create and run events, ticketing, and check-in.
- **Users** — Member accounts, roles, and credits.
- **Bands** — All bands, moderation, and members.
- **Equipment** — Inventory, categories, and loans.
- **Marketing** — Email campaigns and audiences.
- **Inbox** — Contact-form and channel messages.
- **Payments / Credits** — Financial records.
- **Closures** — Facility blackout dates.
- **Help** — Author member-facing help articles.
- **Settings** — System configuration.

Most lists support search and filtering; click a row to open its detail page.
