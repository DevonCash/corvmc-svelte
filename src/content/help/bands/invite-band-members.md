## Inviting members

From the band's **Members** page (owners and admins), you can invite people two
ways:

- **Existing members** — Search the directory by name and send an invitation.
- **By email** — Invite someone who doesn't have an account yet. They'll get an
  invite and can join after signing up with that email.

## What happens next

- Invited members appear under **pending invitations** until they accept.
- People invited by email show as **platform invites awaiting signup**.
- Invitees [accept from their Bands page](/member/help/accept-band-invitation).

## Managing invitations

You can revoke a pending invitation any time from the Members page. See
[managing members](/member/help/manage-band-members) for roles and removals.
