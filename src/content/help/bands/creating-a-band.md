## Creating a band

1. Open **Bands** from the sidebar.
2. Choose **Create Band**.
3. Give it a **name** and a short **bio**.
4. Create it — you become the band's owner.

## Next steps

Once the band exists, from its dashboard you can:

- [Invite members](/member/help/invite-band-members) (existing members or by email).
- [Flesh out the band profile](/member/help/edit-band-profile) — genres, links,
  and visibility.
- [Book practice sessions](/member/help/book-band-session) for the band.

## Roles

- **Owner** — Full control, including deleting the band or transferring ownership.
- **Admin** — Can manage members and book on behalf of the band.
- **Member** — Can see the schedule and band info.

See [managing members](/member/help/manage-band-members) for how roles work in
practice.
