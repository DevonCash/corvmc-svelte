## Deleting a band

Only the **owner** can delete a band. The option lives in the band's **Settings**
page, in the danger zone.

## What happens

Deleting a band:

- **Cancels its future reservations.**
- **Removes all members** from the band.
- Takes down its profile and any Premium page.

This can't be undone.

## Alternatives

- Stepping back but keeping the band going? [Transfer
  ownership](/member/help/transfer-leave-band) instead.
- Just not active right now? You can leave the profile up and simply stop booking.

If you're unsure, contact staff before deleting.
