## Booking for the band

Owners and admins can book practice sessions in the band's name from the band's
**Reservations → New** page.

1. Open the band's **Reservations** and start a new booking.
2. Pick a **date** and an available **start time**.
3. Set the **duration** — the cost and any limits are shown as you adjust.
4. Add **notes** and confirm.

The session is owned by the band and appears on the band dashboard for everyone in
it.

## Recurring band sessions

If your band practices on a schedule, choose a recurrence (weekly, bi-weekly, or
monthly) when booking. Recurring bookings are a
[sustaining-member benefit](/member/help/recurring-reservations).
