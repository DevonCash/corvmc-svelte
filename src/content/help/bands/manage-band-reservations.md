## The band's reservations

The band's **Reservations** page lists all of its sessions, split into **Upcoming**
and **Past** tabs. Each entry shows the date, time, duration, who booked it, and
its status.

## What you can do

- Review what's coming up and who booked it.
- **Cancel** an upcoming or confirmed session (owners and admins).
- Check status badges to see what's scheduled, confirmed, completed, or cancelled.
