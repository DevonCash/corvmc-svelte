## Leaving a band

If you're a member or admin (not the owner), you can leave a band from its
**Members** page. You'll come off the roster and lose access to the band's pages.

## Transferring ownership

Every band has exactly one **owner**. If you're the owner and want to step back —
or hand the band to someone else — transfer ownership:

1. Open the band's **Members** page.
2. Choose **transfer ownership** and pick the member to hand it to.
3. Confirm. They become the owner; you remain on the roster as an admin or member.

Transferring ownership is the way an owner can eventually leave — hand off first,
then leave.
