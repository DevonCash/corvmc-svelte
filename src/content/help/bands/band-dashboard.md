## Band dashboard

Click a band in **My Bands** to open its dashboard — the band's home base. What you
can do here depends on your role in the band.

## On the page

- **Overview** — Member count, your role, and the band's upcoming sessions.
- **Upcoming sessions** — The band's next reservations and their status.
- **Quick links** — Owners and admins get shortcuts to manage members and edit the
  profile.

## What's in the band area

From the band's pages you can:

- [Manage members](/member/help/manage-band-members) and
  [invite new ones](/member/help/invite-band-members).
- [Edit the band profile](/member/help/edit-band-profile).
- [Book](/member/help/book-band-session) and
  [manage reservations](/member/help/manage-band-reservations).
- [Create and manage events](/member/help/band-events).
- On Premium, [build a custom band page](/member/help/page-editor).
