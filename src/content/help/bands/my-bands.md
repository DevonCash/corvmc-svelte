## My Bands

Open **Bands** from the sidebar to see every band you're part of and any pending
invitations.

## On the page

- **Your bands** — Cards for each band you belong to, showing your role. Click one
  to open its dashboard.
- **Invitations** — Bands that have invited you appear here to
  [accept or decline](/member/help/accept-band-invitation).
- **Create Band** — Start a new band of your own.
