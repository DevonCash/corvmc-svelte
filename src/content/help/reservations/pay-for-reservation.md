## Paying for a session

When a reservation has a balance due, its card shows the amount and a way to pay.
Open the reservation and choose to pay.

1. From **Reservations**, open the session with a balance.
2. Review the amount and hourly rate.
3. Complete payment by card through the secure checkout.

## Free hours and credits

If you're a sustaining member, your monthly
[free practice hours](/member/help/practice-credits) are applied first, reducing or
covering the cost automatically. Anything beyond your free hours is charged at the
hourly rate.

## Paying at the space

Some balances can be settled in person — staff can record a cash payment when you
arrive. If you're unsure what's owed, the reservation detail always shows the
current amount due.
