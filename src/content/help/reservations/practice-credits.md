## Free practice hours

Sustaining members receive an allotment of **free practice hours** each month as a
benefit of membership. These cover the cost of practice-space bookings up to your
monthly limit.

## How they're applied

- Your remaining hours for the month are shown on your **Dashboard** and on the
  **Reservations** page.
- When you book or pay for a session, free hours are applied automatically before
  any charge.
- Hours beyond your monthly allotment are billed at the standard hourly rate.

## Where they come from

Free hours are tied to your sustaining membership and refresh each billing cycle.
Staff can also grant bonus hours in some situations. Your balance is tracked as a
running ledger, so you can always see what you have left.
