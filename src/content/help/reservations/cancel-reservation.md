## Cancelling

1. Open **Reservations** from the sidebar.
2. Find the upcoming session and open it.
3. Choose to cancel and confirm.

## Cancellation policy

You can cancel up to **24 hours** before the start time without charge. Late
cancellations may incur a fee, and no-shows may be charged in full.

## Changing a booking

To move a session, cancel the existing one and
[book the new time](/member/help/booking-a-session). If you booked a
[recurring series](/member/help/recurring-reservations), you can cancel a single
occurrence without ending the whole series.

## Refunds

If you'd already paid and cancel within the free window, any charge is reversed.
If you think a charge is wrong, contact staff and they can review and, if
appropriate, refund it.
