## Welcome

CorvMC is a community music space where you can book rehearsal rooms, connect with other musicians, and join bands.

## What You Can Do

- **Book Reservations** — Reserve practice rooms by the hour
- **Join the Directory** — Share your instruments and genres so others can find you
- **Create or Join Bands** — Collaborate with other members
- **Attend Events** — Check out shows and community events
