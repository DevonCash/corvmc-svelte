## Deleting your account

If you no longer want a CorvMC account, you can delete it from **Account** in the
sidebar.

1. Open **Account**.
2. Find the account-deletion option (near the bottom).
3. Confirm in the dialog that appears.

## Before you delete

- **This can't be undone.** Your profile, directory listing, and personal data are
  removed.
- **Settle anything outstanding first.** Cancel upcoming reservations and resolve
  any unpaid balances.
- **Sustaining members** should cancel their subscription through the
  [billing portal](/member/help/manage-billing) first.

If you only want to stop receiving messages, you don't need to delete your
account — adjust your
[notification preferences](/member/help/notification-preferences) and
[email subscriptions](/member/help/email-subscriptions) instead.
