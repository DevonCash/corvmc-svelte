## Your dashboard

The dashboard is the first thing you see when you sign in. It gives you an
at-a-glance view of your week and quick links to the things you do most.

## What's on it

- **Quick links** — Jump straight to _Book a Session_, _Browse Events_, or
  _Manage Membership_.
- **This week** — A summary of your upcoming reservations for the next seven days.
- **Practice credits** — If you're a sustaining member, your remaining free hours
  for the month are shown here.
- **Upcoming events** — A carousel of shows and community events you can attend.
- **Band invitations** — If someone has invited you to a band, a badge appears so
  you can accept or decline.

## Next steps

New here? A good first pass:

1. Fill out your [profile](/member/help/your-profile) so other members can find you.
2. [Book your first practice session](/member/help/booking-a-session).
3. Explore [becoming a sustaining member](/member/help/become-a-sustaining-member)
   to unlock free practice hours.
