## My Loans

Open **Equipment → My Loans** to see everything you've requested or borrowed.

Each loan shows its current status:

- **Pending** — Submitted, waiting on staff approval.
- **Active** — Approved and checked out to you.
- **Returned** — Checked back in.

You'll also see pickup and return dates and any cost estimate for each loan.

## Returning gear

Bring the item back by its return date and staff will check it in. Returning on
time keeps the library stocked for other members and avoids any late fees.
