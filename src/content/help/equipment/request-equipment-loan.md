## Requesting a loan

1. Open **Equipment** from the sidebar and find the item you want.
2. Start a loan request.
3. Choose your **pickup and return dates** and the **quantity**.
4. Add **notes** if there's anything staff should know.
5. Review the estimated cost (or _Free for sustaining members_) and submit.

Your request goes to staff for approval. Once approved, they'll set aside the
specific item for you to pick up.

## Can't find what you need?

Use the **free-form request** option to describe gear that isn't in the catalog.
Staff can follow up if it's something we have or can source.

## Costs

Loan costs depend on the item's pricing tier. Sustaining members may borrow some
gear at no charge; any cost is shown before you submit and can draw on your credit
balance.
