# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: May 2026_

The Corvallis Music Collective ("CMC", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

## What We Collect

- **Account information** — Name, email address, phone number, and pronouns when you create an account.
- **Payment information** — Processed securely by [Stripe](https://stripe.com/privacy). We store only the last four digits of your card and card type for your reference. We never see or store your full card number.
- **Usage data** — Reservation history, event attendance, and equipment loans associated with your account.
- **Contact form submissions** — Name, email, and message content when you use our contact form.
- **Email subscriptions** — Email address and name when you subscribe to our mailing lists.

## How We Use It

- To provide and improve our services (reservations, events, equipment lending, membership).
- To communicate with you about your account, reservations, and events.
- To send marketing emails you've opted into (you can unsubscribe at any time).
- To process payments and manage memberships.

## What We Don't Do

- We **never sell** your personal information.
- We **never share** your data with third parties for their marketing purposes.
- We do not use advertising trackers or third-party analytics that identify you personally.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate:

- **Stripe** — Payment processing ([Stripe Privacy Policy](https://stripe.com/privacy))
- **Postmark** — Transactional and marketing email delivery ([Postmark Privacy Policy](https://postmarkapp.com/privacy-policy))
- **Cloudflare** — Hosting and content delivery ([Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/))

## Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

## Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at [info@corvmc.org](mailto:info@corvmc.org) or through our [contact form](/contact).

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via email to active members.

## Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at [info@corvmc.org](mailto:info@corvmc.org).
