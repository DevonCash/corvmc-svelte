# Bylaws

The Corvallis Music Collective is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization governed by a set of bylaws adopted by the Board of Directors.

Our bylaws cover:

- **Purpose and mission** of the organization
- **Membership** classes and rights
- **Board of Directors** composition, terms, and responsibilities
- **Officers** and their duties
- **Meetings** and voting procedures
- **Finances** and fiscal year
- **Amendments** process

A full copy of our current bylaws is available on request. Please [contact us](/contact) or email [info@corvmc.org](mailto:info@corvmc.org) to request a copy.
