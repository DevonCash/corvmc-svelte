# Help Support Our Mission

The Corvallis Music Collective runs entirely on the energy and generosity of our community. Here are some ways you can help.

## Volunteer Your Time

We're always looking for people who want to pitch in — no musical experience required.

- **Event support** — Help set up, run sound, or manage the door at shows.
- **Social media** — Create content, take photos, or help spread the word.
- **Facility maintenance** — Keep our space in shape for everyone to enjoy.
- **Grant writing** — Help us secure funding to expand our programs.

Interested? [Get in touch](/contact) and let us know what you'd like to do.

## Make a Donation

Every dollar goes directly toward sustaining our programs and space.

- **$25** — Covers supplies for one workshop session
- **$50** — Sponsors a month of open mic nights
- **$100** — Provides a free membership for a musician in need
- **$250** — Funds equipment repairs and upgrades for the practice space

## Other Ways to Help

- **Attend events** — Show up, bring friends, and support local music.
- **Share on social media** — Help us reach more musicians in the area.
- **Become a member** — Your membership directly funds our programs.
- **In-kind donations** — We accept instruments, gear, and materials.

## Donation Wishlist

Some specific items we're looking for right now:

- Window signage for our storefront
- Used turntables in working condition
- Stage paint and supplies
- Workshop equipment (soldering stations, multimeters)

Have something else that might help our music community? [Let us know](/contact).
