# Programs

## Practice Space

Affordable hourly rehearsal rooms available to all members. Our space features sound-treated walls, a full PA system, and backline gear so you can show up and play.

- **$15/hour** for standard members
- **Up to 10 free hours/month** for sustaining members
- Book online through your member dashboard

[Book a Session](/login?redirect=/member)

## Shows & Performances

We host monthly showcases, open mic nights, and collaborative events that create spaces where musicians can grow, collaborate, and celebrate the power of live music.

Whether you're looking to play your first show or bring a touring act through town, we'd love to work with you.

[View Upcoming Events](/events) | [Apply to Perform](/contact)

## Meetups & Clubs

Regular gatherings for musicians to connect, learn, and jam together.

- **Real Book Club** (1st Thursday) — Our flagship jazz jam club, welcoming all skill levels. Bring your instrument and sit in.
- **Songwriter Circle** (2nd Saturday) — Share original compositions in a supportive, feedback-friendly environment.
- **Monthly Meetup** (Last Thursday) — Casual hangs to talk gear, gigs, and the local music scene.

## Gear Lending Library

Members can borrow instruments and equipment from our growing collection — guitars, amplifiers, recording gear, and more.

- **Loan period:** 1–2 weeks, renewable
- **Refundable security deposit** required
- Browse available gear in your member dashboard

[Browse Equipment](/login?redirect=/member/equipment)
